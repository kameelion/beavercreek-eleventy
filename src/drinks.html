6 Rotating Craft Selections

We proudly rotate our craft beer selection regularly, featuring local breweries and seasonal favorites. Ask your server about our current offerings!

Previous selections have included craft beers from Victory, Troegs, Dogfish Head, Yards, and other exceptional breweries.

Bottles also available for take-out. Ask your server for details.

Wine Selection

Red Wines

We offer a curated selection of red wines available by the glass or bottle, including Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Pinot Noir, and Malbec.

Ask your server about our featured wine of the month and special regional selections.

White Wines

Our white wine selection includes Chardonnay, Pinot Grigio, Sauvignon Blanc, and Riesling, available by glass or bottle.

Perfect pairings for our menu items - ask your server for recommendations.

Classic Cocktail Examples

Bloody Mary

Our house specialty with vodka, signature spicy mix, celery, and olive

Old Fashioned

Bourbon, bitters, sugar, and orange peel - a timeless classic

Margarita

Tequila, triple sec, and fresh lime juice - available classic or flavored

Manhattan

Whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters with a cherry garnish

Mojito

White rum, sugar, lime juice, soda water, and fresh mint

Martini

Gin or vodka with dry vermouth - shaken or stirred to your preference

White Russian

Vodka, coffee liqueur (Kahlúa), and cream layered for a smooth, rich flavor

Cosmopolitan

Vodka, cranberry juice, triple sec, and fresh lime juice - a vibrant and refreshing classic
